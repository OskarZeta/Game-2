    Displays the muscular might of your character. Every point of strength gives you +4 damage and +1 max health.

    Determines your quickness in battle. Every point of speed gives you +4 initiative and +0.5 chance to dodge enemy attack.

    Determines the toughness of your character. Every point of strength gives you +4 max health and +0.5% damage resistance.

Use roads and doors to navigate through the levels. Defeat enemies to gain experience and some useful loot. Once you have enough experience, your level will increase, giving you 1 additional LP (level point). You can spend it to raise one of your four main attributes. You can also interact with various items by clicking on them in your inventory (some of them are equippable items, like weapons and armor, and some are disposable, like healing potions).
